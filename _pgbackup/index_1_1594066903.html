Home Sellers

Whether you are ready to list your home tomorrow or in a few years Park Realty can help you plan to get top dollar. We will help you get your home for the market, prepare a marketing plan and discuss pricing strategies. We are committed to our clients and will be there for you each step of the way!

Thinking of selling?

If you're considering selling your home we would be happy to earn your business. Once you're ready to take the first step just reach out to us! During our first meeting we will see your home (don't worry about cleaning up for us!) and more importantly meet you. We can discuss marketing, timeframes and any other questions but we typically won't be able to give you a price tag. After we meet we will do thorough research considering comps and other market factors and draw up a net proceeds chart so you can make the most informed decisions.
